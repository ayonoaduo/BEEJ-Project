Project Description

There are currently three known methods to contact the ministries if citizens have a problem in their area; call in to the relevant ministry, contact through social media, and fill out a form. Not one of these methods have proven to be the most effective. Through our app, we hope to create one streamlined cross platform app that would become the easiest and most effective of communication between the citizens and the government.
